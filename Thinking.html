Thinking Skills

Learning

Actively engage in your OJT by immersing yourself in the tasks and projects assigned to you. Take a proactive approach to learning, ask questions, seek clarification, and demonstrate curiosity. Embrace the opportunity to apply theoretical knowledge to practical situations.

Reasoning

Reasoning skills are foundational for continuous learning and professional development. They equip you with the ability to learn new technologies, adapt to changing industry trends, and stay updated with emerging practices. By honing your reasoning skills, you become a lifelong learner in the dynamic field of IT.

Creative Thinking

Creative thinking enhances your communication and presentation skills. It helps you articulate your ideas, express your thoughts effectively, and present information in a compelling and engaging manner. These skills are vital for conveying your innovative ideas to stakeholders, clients, or colleagues during your OJT and beyond.

Decision Making

Decision-making is an integral part of problem-solving. As an IT student, you'll encounter various challenges that require you to analyze the situation, assess available options, and make informed decisions to resolve the issues effectively.

Problem Solving

Finding innovative solutions and thinking outside the box are frequently necessary for tackling problems. You can develop your capacity for creative thought during your OJT to come up with novel solutions. This makes it possible for you to develop original solutions that tackle problems in novel ways, resulting in outcomes that are more efficient and effective.
